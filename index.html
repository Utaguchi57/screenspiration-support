What it is

Screenspiration keeps a quiet stream of inspiration drifting across your desktop. No Dock clutter, no window to manage — it lives in the menu bar and gently rotates a single translucent card, so a good idea is always one glance away. You choose what inspires you, and how often it changes.

Make it yours

Pick your lists, set the rhythm, and choose from six calm palettes. Pull any idea full-screen with Focus. Save the lines that land as Gems, and let just those rotate. Build your own lists, too.

Where is the app? I don't see a window.
Screenspiration lives in your menu bar (look for the icon near the clock), not the Dock. Click it for the controls and settings.
How do I choose what shows up?
Open Settings from the menu-bar popover. Tick any list under a category — Voices, Proverbs, Words, Virtues, Moves, Science & Nature — to include it in the rotation. You can build your own lists too.
How do I change how often it changes?
Settings → Timing. Pick an interval from ten seconds to a day. There's a separate rhythm for the full-screen Focus view.
How do I hide it for a screenshot or presentation?
Use the hide control in the popover or the Settings title bar. Cards are also excluded from screen recordings and shares by default.
What's Sage?
