FAQ

Where is the app? I don't see a window.
Screenspiration lives in your menu bar (look for the speech-bubble icon), not the Dock. Click it for the controls and settings.
How do I add or remove content?
Open Settings from the menu-bar popover. Each category — Quotes, Proverbs, Words and Ideas, Equations and Formulae — has an Add button, plus Refresh and Randomize. Tick a list to include it in the rotation.
How do I change how often quotes change?
Settings → Timing. There's a separate interval for the full-screen idle screen.
How do I hide it temporarily (for a screenshot or presentation)?
Use the eye button in the popover or the Settings title bar. Quotes are also excluded from screen recordings and shares by default.
Does it work offline / collect any data?
Yes, fully offline, and it collects no data. See the Privacy Policy.
