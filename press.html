What it is

Screenspiration keeps a quiet stream of inspiration drifting across your desktop. It lives in the menu bar — no Dock clutter, no window to manage — and gently rotates a single translucent card so a good idea is always one glance away. You choose what inspires you and how often it changes.

The collections

  • Voices — quotes from thinkers, makers, and doers across history.
  • Proverbs — folk wisdom from world languages, many read aloud in their native tongue.
  • Words — a single word or virtue to sit with.
  • Virtues — timeless qualities drawn from many traditions.
  • Moves — thinking tools and problem-solving prompts to shift your perspective.
  • Science & Nature — the elegance of mathematical formulae and the structures of important molecules.

Why it's different

  • Ambient, not another app to open — it lives in the menu bar and drifts across the desktop.
  • Depth beyond quotes — virtues, problem-solving moves, molecules & equations; curated, not scraped.
  • Genuinely private — no account, no internet, no tracking. Speech is on-device; nothing leaves your Mac.
  • Calm & honest by design — no dark patterns, honest pricing, premium shown by graying out, not locks.

Pricing, precisely

  • Free 14-day trial — everything unlocked, no card required.
  • Free forever — Voices, Words, and Proverbs (English, Spanish, French, Chinese, Latin) + the ambient overlay + the default palette.
  • Sage — $20/year, or $50 once for life — unlocks Virtues, Moves, Science & Nature, the remaining proverb languages, all six palettes, Focus, custom lists, editing, and every future collection.

Privacy

Data not collected. No network calls, no analytics, no third-party SDKs. Sandboxed; on-device speech. See the Privacy Policy.
