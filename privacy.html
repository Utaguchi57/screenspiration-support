Privacy Policy

Screenspiration · Last updated 27 June 2026

Screenspiration collects no data. The app runs entirely on your Mac and is designed to need nothing from you and nothing from the internet.

What we collect

Nothing. Screenspiration has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

Network

The app makes no network connections. It works fully offline.

Your content and settings

Your preferences and any lists you create or import are stored locally in the app's sandbox on your device. They never leave your Mac and are not accessible to us.

Text-to-speech

The optional read-aloud feature uses your Mac's built-in voices on-device. No audio or text is sent anywhere.

Children

Because the app collects no data at all, it is safe for users of any age.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Questions about privacy? Email UtaguchiLabs@gmail.com.
